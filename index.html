CV Publications Talks

I am a postdoctoral research associate in Theoretical Division at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL) in Los Alamos, NM, USA. Before working at LANL, I received his B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2016 and 2019, respectively. I completed his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology in 2023.

My research interests lie in power systems, focusing on developing algorithms and solving advanced optimization problems to enhance the reliability and resiliency of power grids.
